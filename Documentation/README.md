How to play?
    Make
    ./Battle
    Game begins!


9 x 10 board
Columns denoted by letters (A - J)
Rows denoted by numbers (1 - 9)

Taking turns, users pick a space on opponent's board to fire at

Once a player has sunk all of the opponent's ships, they immediately win
